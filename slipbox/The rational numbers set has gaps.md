> [!important] Proposition: The [[Rational Numbers|rational numbers]] set has gaps
> $A=\{p\in\mathbb{Q}^{*+}|p^2<2\},B=\{p\in\mathbb{Q}^{*+}|p^2>2\}\implies\nexists largest(A),smallest(B)$
